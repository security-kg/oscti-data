Slideshow: Intel from Virus Bulletin 2018

Author: Tara Seals
October 9, 2018 2:40 pm

This year’s Virus Bulletin conference featured top-tier research from some of the world’s best threat intelligence experts.

This year’s Virus Bulletin 2018, held in Montreal Oct. 3-5, featured a keynote from Microsoft’s John Lambert and a range of threat intelligence research from BAE Systems, Check Point, Cisco Talos, Citizens Lab, Digita Security, ESET, GoSecure, Kaspersky Lab, Malwarebytes, Sophos Security and more. Over three days, concise presentations offered insight into the top threats on the malware, attack tactics and APT fronts facing individuals and organizations today.
