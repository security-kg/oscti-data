Get a free Android antivirus app: Protect yourself from the latest security hole

Enduser•Mobile
19 July 2013
  • 0
  • Share on Twitter
  • Share on Facebook
  • Share on LinkedIn
By John Zorabedian

Security researchers recently warned Google of a vulnerability in the Android mobile operating system that allows malicious apps to install on your smartphone without triggering any warnings. That’s the bad news. The good news? Our free Mobile Security for Android app scans all the apps you download to keep you protected.

Sophos Mobile Security for Android protects your device without reducing performance or battery life. Using up-to-the-minute intelligence from SophosLabs, it scans your apps on demand or in intervals of your choice.

The antivirus functionality helps you avoid undesirable software. And if your device is lost or stolen, a remote lock or wipe will shield your personal information from prying eyes.

You can get the free app here at Google Play. (And you should always get your Android apps from the Play store for security purposes!)

Sophos Mobile Security is also available as an enterprise version you can manage through our Mobile Control MDM solution. For more information, go to the Mobile Control page.
